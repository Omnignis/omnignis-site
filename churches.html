For Churches

Livestream attendance,
reported for you.

If your church reports livestream viewers to your diocese, stop counting by hand. Connect your Facebook page once, and Omnignis emails your office a clean, presentation-ready Excel report every week — automatically.

How it works

Set it up once. It runs every Sunday.

Sign in

Create your church's secure account in the Omnignis portal — just an email and password.

Connect Facebook

Link your page through Facebook's own secure login. You approve it on Facebook — we never see or store your password.

We pull the numbers

After each Sunday livestream ends, Omnignis automatically collects the exact viewer counts from your page.

Report emailed

A formatted, presentation-ready Excel report lands in your church inbox every Sunday afternoon.

Your account stays yours. The connection uses Facebook's official secure login, so Omnignis only receives a limited access token to read your page's viewer numbers — never your password. That token is stored encrypted, it's used only to build your report, and you can disconnect at any time.

Reach out and I'll get you set up. Self-serve sign-in is on the way — for now, the fastest path is a quick message.
