Services

What we do.

Websites, custom software, and security, handled end to end by our team of engineers. Plus ongoing support plans and a specialized reporting tool for churches.

01 · Software Development

Custom software to automate your day to day processes

Off-the-shelf tools force you to work their way. We build software around your actual process, from a professional website to custom apps and automations that remove the repetitive work.

  • Small business websites. Designed, built, and launched: fast, secure, and easy to keep updated.
  • Custom web apps & client portals. Logins, dashboards, and tools shaped around how your business runs.
  • Workflow automation & scripting. The work you do by hand every week, made automatic and connected to the systems you already use.
  • Automated reporting & dashboards. Your data pulled, formatted, and emailed to you on a schedule, ready to present.
02 · Cybersecurity

Protect your business from cyber threats

Security work grounded in how systems actually get broken into, not a checklist. You get a clear picture of your real risks and a prioritized, plain-English plan to close them.

  • Security assessments. A prioritized, jargon-free picture of your real risks. Start with the Security Snapshot below.
  • Hardening & remediation. The fixes, done: MFA rollout, email security (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), backups, access cleanup, and network configuration.
  • Security awareness training. Practical sessions so your team spots the phishing and scams that actually breach small offices.
  • Personal & home cybersecurity. Accounts, devices, and home networks locked down for families, executives, and anyone with a target on their back.
03 · Ongoing Support

Keep it running, keep it secure

Technology isn't done when it launches. A simple monthly plan keeps your site and systems healthy, and gives you a technical team in your corner whenever a decision comes up.

  • Website care plans. Updates, backups, uptime monitoring, and small changes handled every month, so your site never goes stale.
  • Tech advisor retainer. A few hours a month on call: vendor decisions, reviewing quotes, security questions, and straight answers before you spend money.

A done-for-you reporting tool: connect your Facebook page once, and every week Omnignis pulls your livestream viewer numbers and emails your office a clean, presentation-ready Excel report. Ideal for churches that report attendance to their diocese.

  • Secure, one-time Facebook connection with no manual logins each week
  • Exact viewer counts pulled automatically after each livestream
  • A formatted Excel report emailed to you every Sunday
