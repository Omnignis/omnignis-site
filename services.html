What we do.

Three core focuses, each handled end-to-end. Pick one, or combine them — most clients start in one area and grow from there.

01 — Software

Custom software that fits your business

Off-the-shelf tools force you to work their way. I build software around your actual process — web apps, internal tools, and automations that remove the repetitive work and connect the systems you already use.

  • Custom web apps and client portals (Next.js, modern stack)
  • Workflow automations that cut out manual, repetitive tasks
  • Dashboards and reporting that turn your data into decisions
  • Integrations with payments, messaging, and the APIs you rely on
02 — Cybersecurity

Find the gaps before someone else does

Security work grounded in how systems actually get broken into — not a checklist. You get a clear picture of your real risks and a prioritized, plain-English plan to close them.

  • Security assessments with a prioritized, jargon-free action list
  • System and network hardening to shrink your attack surface
  • Web app and database security reviews
  • Security awareness training so your team spots phishing and scams
03 — Cloud Solutions

Cloud that's reliable, secure, and right-sized

Set up the right way, your cloud should be something you never think about. I handle setup, migration, and security — and keep it from quietly draining your budget.

  • Cloud setup and migration done without downtime drama
  • Database and backend design (Supabase, Postgres, and more)
  • Securing cloud environments and access controls
  • Cost reviews that stop runaway bills before they start
