About

Built by someone who builds it
and defends it.

Omnignis isn't a faceless agency. It's a focused practice run by an engineer who'd rather solve your problem well than sell you something you don't need.

Omnignis started on a simple idea: most businesses don't need more technology. They need the right technology, configured correctly and explained honestly. Too many people get sold expensive tools they never use, and security advice they can't act on.

The practice is run by Chris Robinson, a security engineer based in Pinehurst, Texas, with a master's in computer science (cybersecurity concentration) from Boston University. That mix means thinking like a builder and a defender at the same time: the software Omnignis writes is secure by default, and the security work is grounded in how systems actually get broken into.

You work directly with the person doing the work. When you hire Omnignis, you're not handed off to a call center or a script. You get straight answers, fixed prices, and results you can actually understand.
