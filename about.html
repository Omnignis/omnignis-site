About

An engineer who builds it
and secures it.

Omnignis isn't a faceless agency. It's one experienced engineer who'd rather solve your problem well than sell you something you don't need.

I started Omnignis on a simple idea: most businesses don't need more technology — they need the right technology, built correctly and explained honestly. Too many people get sold expensive tools they never use, and security advice they can't act on.

My work pairs a master's in computer science with hands-on experience in software development and security. That mix means I think like a builder and a defender at the same time — so the software I write is secure by default, and the security work I do is grounded in how systems actually get broken into.

I work directly with every client. When you hire Omnignis, you're not handed off to a junior team or a script — you talk to the person doing the work, get straight answers, and see results you can actually understand.
